Our Beginnings

Matilde de Jesús Orrego

The Mis Corazones Alegres Foundation, located in the Prado sector, finds its origin after the disappearance of another center for the care of the elderly, which operated in the sector for 13 years. Given the difficult situation of the elderly community and the enormous need they suffered, three women were given the task of providing protection and asylum to them, which required medical, health, experiential and, above all, affective care. This is how the Foundation began work in November 2007, under the guidance of Mrs. Matilde de Jesús Orrego, a newly retired auxiliary nurse, with enormous experience and a great charisma of service, forged during a lifetime of surrender to others; In the company of her husband Gustavo Rengifo, her daughter Isabel Cristina Rengifo Orrego and María Dulfay Vargas.
